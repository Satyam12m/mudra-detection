Under the Hood

How it works

Three stages, running in real time.

01
Hand detection
MediaPipe locates your hand and extracts 21 landmarks — the joints and fingertips — as (x, y, z) coordinates.
02
Feature extraction
Landmarks are normalised relative to the wrist, producing 63 scale-invariant features that describe hand shape alone.
03
Classification
A Random Forest trained on 32,956 samples predicts the mudra and returns a confidence score in milliseconds.
